The Philosophy

The Best Thinking Rarely Happens Under Fluorescent Lights

At However We Got Here, we believe some of the most meaningful breakthroughs — the kind that reframe challenges, uncover new opportunities, or build lasting trust — come when we step outside the usual environment.

That's why we offer curated Client Immersion Experiences, affectionately known as Red Squirrel Outdoors, as an intentional part of how we deepen relationships and move work forward.

What We Do

Experiences at Red Squirrel Outdoors

🎣

Fly Fishing Retreats

Quiet water, intentional conversation, and the rhythm of the cast. We are a licensed fly fishing guide in the State of Alaska — and depending on the season, we fish some of the most remarkable waters in the world.

🚵‍♂️

Mountain Biking

For clients who crave movement and challenge, we explore curated trails that match skill levels and desired intensity. These rides spark conversation, test limits, and shake loose stagnant thinking.

🥾

High-Altitude Hiking

Some of our best strategic sessions take place one step at a time. From alpine lakes to forested paths, hikes create a natural space to ask better questions — and hear better answers.

🍷

Culinary Retreats

Relationships often deepen over food. We've partnered with local chefs and foragers to host intimate dining experiences, cooking sessions, and farm-to-table tastings that complement the work we're doing together.

🧘

Meditation & Mindfulness

Stillness matters. We offer guided sessions and quiet environments for clients looking to reset and return to the work with a clear head and renewed energy.

Important Disclosure

These experiences are not the core of what we do — they are an intentional complement to the consulting relationship. They are designed to foster clarity, collaboration, and trust, and are built into client engagements based on mutual fit and the goals of the partnership. These are invite-only and not available for standalone purchase.
