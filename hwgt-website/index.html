About Mike

Every role. Every level. Every size deal.

He started dialing into accounts that had never heard of the company. No brand recognition, no warm leads, no territory. He learned to get meetings where there were no meetings to get, and worked deals where the buyer had no budget, no timeline, and no reason to call back. Then he built a whitespace territory in a rural market from nothing and closed it out.

Then he ran teams. Built a global BDR organization from 3 to 11 reps and grew pipeline from $2M to $20.8M. Took a product with under $100K in ARR and scaled it to over $10M. Along the way: transactional SaaS deals worth a couple thousand dollars and multi-million dollar enterprise closings. Tens of millions in pipeline built cold. The smallest SMB accounts and Global 500 enterprise accounts. Every quota structure. Every comp plan. Every kind of deal that can fall apart at the one-yard line.
