Why This Exists

Nobody coached me.
I am fixing that.

When I was a BDR at Compellent making cold calls into enterprise accounts nobody had heard of, I did not have anyone to ask. No mentor, no coach. Just trial and error. This is the resource I wish had existed.

Why coaching and not a course

Sales courses teach frameworks. The problem is your prospect did not read the framework. They have a specific objection, a specific org chart, a specific timeline, and the framework does not cover the specific thing you are stuck on right now.

Coaching works on the actual situation in front of you. You bring your deal, your call, your real problem. We talk through it. That is different from a module on handling objections that applies to nobody in particular.

Who this is for

BDRs who want to get better before anyone notices they need to. AEs who are carrying real quota and do not have time for advice that does not apply to their deal. Reps who are good at their jobs and want to get better at the parts that are still hard.

Mike got lucky. The managers he worked for actually taught him things: how to think through a deal, how to handle a hard conversation, how to get better. Not everyone gets that. This is his way of passing it on.
