The Honest Assessment

Most Trades Companies Are One Good Sales System Away From Doubling Revenue

Whether you're a SaaS platform that grew up serving power washers, roofers, or fencing companies — or you are that fencing company — the problem is usually the same: great service delivery, weak sales infrastructure.

Reps who rely on gut instinct instead of process. Leads falling through the cracks because the CRM is an afterthought. No organic pipeline, so you're hooked on expensive paid lead gen. Sales managers who are really just senior reps with a title.

These aren't character flaws — they're the natural result of companies that scaled by doing great work, not by building great systems. We fix the systems.

"The best trades companies I've seen don't win because they're the cheapest or even the best. They win because they never let a qualified lead go cold."

Sales Process Audit & Optimization

A full diagnostic of your revenue engine — from first touch to closed deal. We find the leaks, rank them by impact, and give you a prioritized roadmap to fix them. No fluff, no 90-page slide decks.

  • Proposal and follow-up cadence teardown
  • Clear, prioritized action plan delivered within 2 weeks

Sales Rep Coaching & Team Development

Most construction and trades reps have never had a real coach. We change that. We work directly with your reps and managers to build the skills, habits, and accountability structures that compound over time.

  • 1:1 rep coaching tied to real deal reviews
  • Sales manager enablement
  • Objection handling frameworks built for your specific buyers

  • Hiring profile development for your next rep

Organic Growth Strategy

Paid lead gen is a tax on a weak content strategy. We help you build the organic infrastructure that drives warm leads.

  • Local SEO and Google Business optimization for trades

  • Reduce cost-per-lead while improving lead quality

CRM & Sales Tech Stack Buildout

Most trades companies have a CRM they don't trust. We design and implement the right setup for your stage.
