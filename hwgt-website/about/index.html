About Mike

From BDR to CRO —
every step of the way.

Started cold-calling enterprise storage deals. Ended up Chief Revenue Officer. Held every seat in between.

I didn't have a coach.

I started at Dell Compellent making cold calls into enterprise accounts. No script. No territory map. No one who'd been through it to tell me what to actually do. I figured it out through trial and error — a lot of error — and eventually got good enough that Dell acquired Compellent and kept me on.

After Dell I took a territory role at Cambridge Computer. Rural Michigan and northern Wisconsin. Nobody owned it. No installed base. Just whitespace and a phone. I built it from scratch, finished top 15% in revenue attainment among new reps that year, and hit 122% of quota.

That path took me through VMware on the enterprise side, then into startups — Blue Medora, Integration Plumbers — where I ran sales when the whole org chart fit in one room. Eventually I was running revenue as CRO.

What I'm doing now is what I wish existed when I was starting out: honest, practical coaching for BDRs and AEs who are serious about getting better. No course. No framework you have to buy into. Just real conversation about real situations.
